Concerts

A concert is a live music performance in front of the audience.The performance may be by a single musician, sometimes then called a recital,such as an orchestra, choir, or band. Concerts are held in a wide variety and size of settings, from private houses and small nightclubs, dedicated concert halls, theatres and parks, to large multipurpose buildings, such as arenas and stadium.

Food Festival

A food festival is a festival, that uses food, often produce, as its central theme(attraction). These festivals have always been a means of uniting communities through celebrations of harvests and giving thanks for a plentiful growing season. Food festivals are quickly becoming part of a vast food tourism industry. Food tourism itself has become an important part of the tourism industry, and the presence of food festivals shown to support local industry development. Food tourism is also an important reason why people attend food festivals around the world. Studies have shown that engagement with food tourism indicates that an individual will attend festivals again in the future, indicating a cooperative element to food tourism and food festival attendance.
